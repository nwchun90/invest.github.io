The DRIP compounding effect
Extra value generated by DRIP
—
Dividends automatically reinvested each month purchased additional units at prevailing prices, growing your share count from — to — units. Without DRIP, the same price appreciation would have produced only —.
Dividend income collected (cumulative)
—
Total gross dividends generated over 5 years — entirely reinvested into new units. VDY pays monthly, meaning 60 individual reinvestments occurred, each buying fractional units that compound forward. The dividend per unit grew from ~C$0.109/month (2021) to ~C$0.195/month (2026 Q1) — a 79% increase in per-unit income.
